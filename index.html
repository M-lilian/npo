Homepage & Navigation

Needs Fix

Branding

The **NPO TER logo is missing** from the homepage and overall site header.

Navigation

Overall user navigation is not intuitive, making it hard to move around the site.

Forms & Translation

Needs Fix

Contact Form Input Validation

The Contact Form accepts blank or improperly formatted data (e.g., a fake email address) before showing the main connection error.

**Recommendation:** Implement front-end checks to instantly warn users if they left a field blank or typed a wrong email format.

Needs Fix

Newsletter Signup

The signup box on the About page doesn't clearly state what to submit. **Nothing happens** after submission.

**Recommendation:** Add clear labels (e.g., "Email Address"). Display a confirmation message (e.g., "Thank you for subscribing!").

Needs Fix

Translation Feature

The translation widget appears to default to French or has a French tag that reads: *"Provided by Google Translate."*

**Recommendation:** Set the intended language default and apply styling to hide or correctly format the translation provider text.

ðŸ“ˆ Next Steps: Planning for Stability & Growth

The critical breakdowns confirm our immediate focus must be on **fixing the donation and contact processes first**. Once the core is stable, we will proceed with the UX and content improvements.

Following these essential repairs, we will move to the advanced testing phases, which directly influence your online visibility and reputation.

  • 1. Performance & Speed

    We will measure exactly how fast the pages load. This is critical for users who browse on their phones or have slower internet connections.

    Business Impact: Reduces frustration and visitor "bounce-rate." Faster sites rank better on search engines.

  • 2. Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

    We will check if the website is structured correctly to be easily found by Google and other search engines.

    Business Impact: Increases free traffic (donors, volunteers, partners) who are searching for your mission.

  • 3. Security

    We will test for vulnerabilities to ensure user data (like contact info) is safe and the site is protected from malicious attacks.

    Business Impact: Protects your organization's reputation and ensures legal compliance (e.g., with privacy laws).

  • 4. Compatibility

    We will test the site on different browsers (Chrome, Safari, Firefox) and devices (phones, tablets) to ensure it looks and works perfectly for everyone.

    Business Impact: Ensures a positive experience for ALL users, regardless of their device.
